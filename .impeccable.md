## Design Context

### Users
This product is used by developers, operators, and administrators who manage a local AI proxy service. They use it to maintain upstream accounts, platform keys, routing rules, request logs, and system safety status for a self-hosted gateway.

### Brand Personality
Calm, technical, trustworthy. The interface should feel controlled and professional rather than flashy, playful, or consumer-social. The emotional goal is confidence: the user should feel they are operating a dependable local control plane.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode first, with cool blue as the anchor hue and softly tinted neutrals. The visual language should communicate cloud AI, proxy access, security boundaries, and operational clarity. Prefer cloud-and-intelligence symbolism over terminal prompts or overly industrial gateway marks. Avoid generic "AI neon" cues, mascots, and decorative sci-fi gradients.

### Design Principles
1. Make infrastructure feel understandable, not intimidating.
2. Prefer crisp operational symbols over illustrative decoration.
3. Use restrained brand color with clean, high-contrast structure.
4. Keep layouts calm and data-first, with subtle polish instead of spectacle.
5. Let cloud and intelligence motifs appear through geometry, not slogans.
